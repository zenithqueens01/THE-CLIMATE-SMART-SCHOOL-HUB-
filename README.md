## 🌱 Overview

The **Climate Smart School Hub (CSSH)** is a scalable, child-centered platform that transforms schools into resilient learning, health, and community response centers across climate-vulnerable regions in Africa.

CSSH integrates:
- 🌦 Climate education and early warning systems
- ❤️ School health readiness and monitoring tools
- ☀️ Sustainable and resilient infrastructure models
- 📱 Offline-first digital tools for low-connectivity environments

The platform is designed to ensure **uninterrupted learning and child safety** during climate emergencies, disease outbreaks, and environmental crises — with a primary focus on under-resourced schools and communities in Nigeria and across Africa.

> *"Every child deserves a safe school, clean water, and a future not defined by climate crisis."*

---

## ⚠️ Problem Statement

Climate change is increasingly disrupting education across vulnerable communities. Flooding, extreme heat, disease outbreaks, poor infrastructure, and lack of access to clean water and reliable energy are directly threatening children's safety, health, and learning continuity.

### The Gaps CSSH Addresses:

| Challenge | Impact |
|---|---|
| No early warning systems | Schools are unprepared for climate disasters |
| Absent climate education | Children lack knowledge to adapt and respond |
| Weak health readiness | Disease outbreaks go undetected and unmanaged |
| Poor infrastructure | Buildings collapse; no clean water or energy |
| Digital exclusion | Rural schools can't access online learning tools |
| Community disconnection | No coordinated emergency response at school level |

These challenges disproportionately affect millions of children in climate-vulnerable communities across Nigeria and Sub-Saharan Africa. CSSH addresses all of these through one integrated, scalable platform.

## 🏛️ System Architecture

The CSSH platform integrates schools, communities, health systems, and climate-response tools into one unified ecosystem.

```
                    ┌─────────────────────────────┐
                    │     Central Cloud Dashboard  │
                    └──────────────┬──────────────┘
                                   │
        ┌──────────────────────────┼──────────────────────────┐
        │                          │                           │
┌───────▼────────┐      ┌──────────▼─────────┐    ┌──────────▼─────────┐
│  Early Warning │      │  Health Monitoring  │    │  Climate Learning  │
│     System     │      │      System         │    │       Hub          │
└───────┬────────┘      └──────────┬─────────┘    └──────────┬─────────┘
        │                          │                           │
        └──────────────────────────┼──────────────────────────┘
                                   │
                    ┌──────────────▼──────────────┐
                    │  Mobile & Offline Application│
                    └──────────────┬──────────────┘
                                   │
                    ┌──────────────▼──────────────┐
                    │  School Infrastructure       │
                    │  (Solar, WASH, Shelter)      │
                    └─────────────────────────────┘
```

### Core Components:
- **Central Cloud Dashboard** — Aggregates all school and community data
- **Early Warning System** — Monitors and broadcasts climate/health alerts
- **Health Monitoring System** — Tracks student health and outbreak patterns
- **Mobile & Offline Application** — Operates without internet connectivity
- **Climate Learning Hub** — Delivers interactive education content
- **School Infrastructure Systems** — Manages sustainable energy and water

To run the project locally:

## 🌍 Scalability Vision

CSSH is engineered for phased, scalable deployment:

